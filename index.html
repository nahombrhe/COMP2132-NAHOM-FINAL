Rules:

Fighter Dice is a game of dice against a computer.

You will pick your respective characters, select a map, and begin.

Two dice are rolled each, for a combined sum each round.

If the player rolls a 1 in either die, an automatic 0 is given for that round.

If two of the same dice are rolled, the total for that round is doubled.

All rules apply to the computer as well.

Three rounds, highest total score at the end wins.
